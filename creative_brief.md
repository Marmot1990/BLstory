## Setting: The Wasteland Workshop
- **Location**: Underground bunker in a post-apocalyptic wasteland (likely former industrial zone)
- **Atmosphere**: Dim lighting, rusted metal surfaces, rain sounds outside, dust particles floating in light beams
- **Key Props**: 
  - Rusted metallic rings (inscribed with "Sync" symbol)
  - Recycled leather restraint straps
  - Temperature contrast tools: ice packs + small heater units
  - Leather chair with mounting points for restraints
  - Iron cage / workshop fixture with locking mechanism

## Chapter 1 Launch Parameters
- **Scene**: Vex's private studio/workshop, late night (23:00+), heavy rain falling outside
- **Trigger Event**: Nyx accidentally stumbles into the space while lost in the wasteland
- **Initial Dynamic**: Vex → gentle dominance × Nyx → passive acceptance with occasional resistance
- **Word Count Target**: ~5,000-6,000 Chinese characters for Chapter 1
- **Sensory Focus**: 
  - Auditory: rain sounds, metallic clicks, low electronic tones, heartbeat rhythms
  - Tactile: cool metal + warm breath, rough leather vs. smooth skin, fabric texture contrasts
  - Visual: shadows in dim light, amber pupil contraction, silver-gray highlights
  - Olfactory: leather scent, dust, faint ozone/rust notes
